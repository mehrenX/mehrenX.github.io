Overview

This project showcases a self-portrait intertwined with the essence of my Cancer zodiac sign. Using detailed hand embroidery techniques and a variety of stitches, I incorporated symbolic elements like the crab and crescent moon, reflecting the emotional depth and connection to astrology. The piece merges personal identity with celestial inspiration, resulting in a unique and artistic representation.

Objectives

  • Explore Personal Identity
  • Blend Art and Symbolism
  • Master Embroidery Techniques
  • Express Emotional Depth
  • Experiment with Mixed Media
  • Enhance Artistic Skills
